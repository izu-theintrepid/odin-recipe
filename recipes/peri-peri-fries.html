Peri Peri Fries


HOME

DESCRIPTION

cruncy and as well as soft fries gets introduced to flavour town i.e. the indian spices

INGREDIENTS

  • potatoes
  • cornflour
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • peri peri mixture

STEPS

  1. Cut the potatoes in cuboid shape
  2. heat your oil and fry until a nice light crisp
  3. dip your fries in cornflour water and fry again
  4. afte cooling them for few seconds
  5. sprinkle peri peri mix and salt and shake for a minute and enjoy your tasty fries!!
