MASALA CHAI


HOME

DESCRIPTION

Soothing hot beverage with herbs having many health benefits

INGREDIENTS

  • tea leaves
  • water
  • milk
  • sugar
  • ginger
  • cloves
  • cardamom

STEPS

  1. Boil water with ginger for few minutes before adding tea leaves
  2. add tea leaves while continously mixing with a spoon
  3. add your cloves and cardomom and let it boil
  4. add the lukewarm milk in the mixture
  5. use a tea strainer and uplift your mood by drinking hot beverages even in summers
