HOME

DESCRIPTION

A flavourful moist patty sandwiched between soft toasted buns with vibrant freshness from sliced cucumbers, tomatoes, lettuce, onion and a bright mayo dressing or any other kinds of sauces.

INGREDIENTS

  • potatoe patty
  • buns
  • tomatoes
  • onions
  • cucumber
  • lettuce
  • preffered sauces

STEPS

  1. Take your potatoe patty and fry it
  2. Make sure your buns are cut
  3. Cut your vegetables into an appropiate sizes according to you
  4. Take the bottom bun and spread your first layer of sauce on it
  5. Put a layer of onion and lettuce
  6. Drop the patty with a bit of seasoning
  7. Add your second layer of sauce on the patty
  8. Put the rest of the vegetables i.e. cucumber and tomatoes
  9. Finally close your masterpiece with the top bun
